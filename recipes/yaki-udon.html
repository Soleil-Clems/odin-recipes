Yaki Udon

Description

Yaki Udon is a classic Japanese stir-fry dish with udon noodles and lots of vegetables in a soy-based sauce. Feel free to add meat or tofu.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon packed light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil

How to make it

  1. Whisk together oyster sauce, soy sauce, mirin, rice vinegar, Worcestershire, sesame oil, sugar, Sriracha, and garlic in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add udon noodles and remaining 1/4 cup water and cook, gently separating noodles with tongs or spoon.
